DAY 2: DRAW YOUR MAP

 

Remember: it doesn’t need to be a beautiful work of art, as long as it’s useful to you as you write. As long as it prevents you from getting lost in your world.

 

This will become your absolute go-to piece of information, so it’s important that you keep it beside you as you write, and update it as you need to.

 

You can either draw this in hard copy, or create a digital map. Just make sure you keep everything together, and by your side as you write.
